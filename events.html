School Programs

This is where the school held a program or events for every year so all students can participate and show their own talents.

School Pageants

This event is held every year for the students to join and experience new things around them. This pageant show that every students joining it will gain a little bit of confidence towards themselves and to also show their greatest or hidden talents.

School Foundation Day with Ethnic Attires

Every year, the school celebrates its respective foundation days to commemorate the day when the school was founded or established. Commemorating the school foundation day is significant since it is the chance to remind the students about the humble beginning of their educational institution. Where the school also make it an ethnic attire program for the students to wear their ethnics attire for the event to be more fun.

School Bodybuilding Contest

Unlike strength or endurance, bodybuilding are not focused on performance. It's about enhancing the physical appearance. This events if to shows more confidents about yourself by being healthy. This is always celebrated every year during the nutrition month program where students participate to win or to show their bodies and encourages students to also be healthy.
